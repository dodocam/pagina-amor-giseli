# **App Name**: Amore Nostro

## Core Features:

- Story Slides: Displays a series of romantic stories and memories in a visually appealing, scrollable format.
- Photo Gallery: Showcases a gallery of memorable photos with captions.
- Background Music: Plays a background love song automatically in a loop to enhance the emotional experience.
- AI Love Note Generator: AI tool to generate a short poem or love note based on user-provided keywords (e.g., "first date," "favorite memory," "inside joke").

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft rose (#E75480) to evoke love and affection.
- Background color: Very light pink (#FAF0F4), almost white, to provide a gentle backdrop.
- Accent color: Lavender (#E6E6FA) for subtle highlights and interactive elements.
- Body and headline font: 'Belleza' (sans-serif) for headings and shorter text; 'Alegreya' (serif) for longer body text.
- Use delicate, hand-drawn-style icons to represent key moments or themes in the stories.
- Employ gentle fade-in and slide-up animations for each story section to create a smooth, engaging experience.
- Utilize a full-screen, vertical scrolling layout with scroll-snap to ensure each story section is clearly presented and easily navigable.
